////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

                                           Escrow Flow
                                           
////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

1. Seller creates an order

2. Buyer funds the escrow
    - Pays:
        - Product price
        - Bond (20% of product price)
        - Fee (1% of product price)
    2.1 The buyer can cancel the order at this stage.
        - All funds are refunded to the buyer except the fee.

3. Seller funds the escrow
    - BTR mode:
        - Bond: 20% of product price
        - Fee: 1%
    - STR mode:
        - Bond: 120% of product price
        - Fee: 1%

4. Seller ships the item
    - Physical product:
        - The seller must mark the item as shipped within <Shipping hours>
    - Digital product:
        - The seller must upload the file and press Send (mark as shipped) within <Shipping hours>
    4.1 The seller can cancel the order during the shipping phase.
    4.2 If the seller fails to mark as shipped before the deadline:
        - 20% of the product price is deducted from the seller’s bond
        - Half is given to the buyer as compensation
        - Half is sent to the platform fee wallet
        - All buyer funds in escrow are fully refunded

5. After shipment (Review Phase)
    The buyer has 24 hours to:
        - Confirm the order
        - Open a dispute
    5.1 If the buyer confirms
        - Buyer bond is returned
        - Product payment is released to the seller
        - Seller bond is returned
    5.2 If the buyer takes no action (timeout)
        - Product payment is automatically released to the seller
        - All bonds are returned
    5.3 If the buyer opens a dispute
        - The seller has 24 hours to respond.
        5.3.1 If the seller does not respond (buyer wins)
            - 20% of the product price is deducted from the seller’s bond
            - Half is given to the buyer as compensation
            - Half is sent to the platform fee wallet
            - All buyer funds are refunded
        5.3.2 If the seller responds (discussion phase)
            Both parties have 24 hours to negotiate.
                - Seller can press Refund during dispute
                - Buyer can press Pay seller
            5.3.2.1 Seller refunds
                - All funds are returned to both parties
                - No penalties
            5.3.2.2 Buyer pays seller
                - Buyer bond is returned
                - Product payment is released to the seller
                - Seller bond is returned
            5.3.2.3 If no action after 24 hours (draw)
                - The dispute resolves as a draw
                - All escrowed funds are sent to the platform fee wallet
